Privacy Policy

Introduction

  • We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy details our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your information when you use our Booking Application.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We collect personal details like your name and email address during the booking or registration process.
  • Payment Data: All payment transactions are managed via Stripe. We do not store your credit card or payment information on our systems, but Stripe might collect and retain pertinent payment details for the completion of transactions.

Use of Your Information

  • To facilitate and process your room bookings.
  • To communicate with you regarding your bookings, including sending automatic emails related to booking confirmations or cancellations.
  • To enhance our application and ensure a smooth booking experience for our users.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We strictly use your data for the purposes of managing and facilitating bookings. Your personal data is not sold, traded, or disclosed to third parties, except for Stripe, which manages payment processing on our behalf. We may also disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal requests.

Your Rights

  • You can access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. If you have concerns or questions about how your data is handled, please reach out to us.

Contact Us

  • For any concerns or questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
